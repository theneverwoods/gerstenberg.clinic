BioTE

Elite Health

Elite Health members achieve peak physical and mental performance and improved sexual vitality while reducing their risk of heart disease, stroke and other age-related diseases - all without the program getting in the way of their demanding lifestyle. Your physician and clinical team will create a fully personalized program to help you best manage aging through the Cenegenics method.

Peak Physical and Mental Health

  • Increase Physical and Sexual Vitality
  • Decrease Body Fat Percentage (study shows an average decrease of 30%)*
  • Increased Muscle Tone
  • Improved Mental Sharpness
  • Enhanced Sleep Quality

Decrease Risk of Age Related Disease

  • DXA Scan - evaluates body composition and bone mineral density
  • Vo2 Max - measures cardio-pulmonary efficiency to better plan your exercise
  • Carotid Intima-Media Scan - measures the health of your arteries
  • Telomere Health - measures how well your individual cells are aging
  • Specialized cardiac risk measurements - identifies inflammation, oxidative stress, and genetic mutations leading to vascular disease
