K. Paul Gerstenberg, D.O. received his premedical degree from Lamar University in his hometown Beaumont, TX. He then went on to the University of North Texas Health Sciences Center at Fort Worth to receive his Doctor of Osteopathy degree in 1993. He served his residency at St. Anthony Hospital in Oklahoma City, then went to serve three years in the United States Air Force at Barksdale AFB, Louisiana. After completing his tour of duty, he stayed in Shreveport, Louisiana for two years in private practice. In 2001, he had the opportunity to relocate his wife and five children to Nederland, Texas.

Dr. Gerstenberg practices the full realm of family practice, from newborn care to senior care. He has a great interest in preventative medicine, including osteopathic manipulation when needed, and enjoys working with pediatric and adolescent concerns. He offers traditional medical care, but loves to provide nutritional and alternative approaches when his patients are interested, specifically through gerstenberg.clinic WELLNESS.

Dr. Gerstenberg serves as Port Neches, Texas’ local health authority. He was approached in 2015 to step into this role and stays apprised of current medical and public health concerns, such as Zika virus updates, influenza trends and public health issues that may affect our community.

In addition to her academic credentials, Brandi has extensive clinical experience. She began her career in health care by working on the pediatric floor of Christus St. Mary hospital in Port Arthur when she was only 18 years old. Since that time, she has gained experience in critical care/ICU, emergency room, cardiac care, pediatrics, neurology, oncology, and orthopedics.

Her scope of practice encompasses health promotion, disease prevention, diagnosis and management of common and complex health care problems from childhood to seniority.

Brandi has a strong core of bedside nursing, along with advanced assessment and critical thinking skills, which help her comprehensively evaluate every patient by taking time to listen and understand their particular needs.
