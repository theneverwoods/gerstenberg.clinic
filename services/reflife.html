What makes the
"Medical Weight Loss" program "medical"?

It is important to understand that the Medical Weight Loss program is not merely a diet. It is a medically designed protocol that utilizes a combination of very specific foods at 500 calories per day and the use of HCG to change the way your body processes food. Read more on HCG below.

What is HCG?

HCG is an abbreviation for Human Chorionic Gonadotropin. It is a hormone that is naturally produced at very high levels in a women’s body during pregnancy, causing her body to metabolize fat and use it as energy for both mother and baby. Further, it ONLY targets abnormal fat reserves (which are normally the last fat reserves to be used for fuel).

Men may naturally produce HCG in “microscopic” amounts. However, many safe and proven uses have been reported in men. Thus, men and women process HCG exactly the same way, targeting abnormal fat reserves to be used as energy.

Trouble areas like the double chin, potbelly and fat around the hips and thighs are the first to go, while structural and essential fats stay untouched.

This Isn't Just Another Fad

The use of HCG as a diet aid was first discovered in the 1950’s by British Physician A.T.W. Simeons, who devoted 30 years of his career to the exploration of weight loss treatments.

Dr. Simeons observed that his patients could drastically reduce caloric intake without experiencing irritability, hunger pains, or weakness while on HCG.

As Dr. Simeons continued his research, he found that HCG did more than just help his patients lose weight; it also enabled natural reshaping of the body without engaging in exercise.
