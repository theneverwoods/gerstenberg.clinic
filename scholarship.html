Requirements of Students:

  • From Southeast Texas, Jefferson or Orange counties.
  • Human Healthcare Sciences student, either current major or major of interest for highschool seniors. See below for examples.
  • GPA of at least 3.0 A transcript will be requested upon selection of finalists via email.
  • Education level: Highschool seniors, undergrad or graduatate programs.

Submission Requirements:

Record a video answering at least one of the following questions:

  • How do you plan to contribute to the medical community?
  • What is a developing medical therapy or treatment that you find interesting?
  • How have you grown as a person through your personal health journey?
  • How have personal experiences affected your career path choice?

Upload your video to YouTube or Facebook, or Instagram.

Tag "@gerstenberg.clinic" or "@gdotc" on your upload.

Include URL linking to your video in your scholarship submission.

Does it hurt?

Just like getting your blood drawn, there will be a small stick. But once the IV is flowing, the needle is removed, and you just have a small piece of plastic as a conduit to your vein.

You may be slightly cold, so come mentally prepared for that.

How quickly will I see results?

It depends - on you and the treatment you receive. People coming in to treat inflammation or illness often feel better immediately. But for chronic issues, repeated treatments are recommended before expecting to see results.

Who can receive IV infusions?

Almost anyone. You will need to consult with a provider beforehand to determine the best treatment option for you.
