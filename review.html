On Your Phone

  • After you click the link, your phone should redirect to this screen, of our Google My Business listing.
  • If you don't see it at first, scroll a bit - it may be slightly down.
  • Tap the "Reviews" tab under our listing.

On Your Computer

  • After you click the link, your phone should redirect to this screen, of our Google My Business listing.
  • A modal should pop up where you can type your review, or sign into your Google account.
  • If this doesn't work, just click the "Reviews" tab under our listing.
